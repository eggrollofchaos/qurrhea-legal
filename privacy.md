# Qurrhea Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Qurrhea (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a private career assistant application.

## 1) What we collect
We collect only the information needed to provide the app:

- Account/authentication data (via Clerk or connected identity providers).
- User-provided files and content (e.g., resumes, job preferences, notes, outreach notes).
- Application workflow data (saved jobs, statuses, generated drafts, audit events).
- If you connect LinkedIn: basic profile/account data and tokens needed for authorized access.
- Technical logs required for reliability and security.

## 2) How we use data
We use data to:

- authenticate users,
- personalize job matching and draft generation,
- support application workflow and outreach tracking,
- maintain security, debugging, and service reliability.

We do **not** sell personal data.

## 3) LinkedIn data
If you connect LinkedIn, data is used only for features you explicitly enable (e.g., profile sync and outreach-related workflows).  
LinkedIn data is shown only within your account context and is not shared with third parties except processors required to run the service.

## 4) Data sharing
We share data only with service providers needed to operate Qurrhea (e.g., hosting, database, auth, AI APIs), under contractual protections.

## 5) Retention
We retain data while your account is active or as needed to operate the service and meet legal obligations.  
You may request deletion of account data.

## 6) Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls and encryption at rest where supported by our infrastructure providers.

## 7) Your choices
You can:

- update profile data in the app,
- disconnect linked providers (such as LinkedIn),
- request deletion of your account data.

## 8) International processing
Data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

## 9) Children
Qurrhea is not intended for children under 13.

## 10) Changes
We may update this policy. We will post the updated date above.

## 11) Contact
For privacy requests, contact: `privacy@qurrhea.com`
(If you do not have a domain mailbox yet, use a monitored email you control.)
